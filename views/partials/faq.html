How do I get started?

Getting started is easy, all you need is some TF2 or VGO keys. When you're ready, entered your desired sell amount on the 'sell' section on the left hand side, and click on the sell button.

Please check all trade offers before accepting them, and make sure the values on the offer's message are correct.

After accepting, your account balance will be credited within 20 seconds. You can now use your balance to purchase TF2 or VGO keys. Use the OPSkins cashout feature to cashout your remaining balance!

Can I use a custom OPSkins account?

Yes you can! You may edit your SteamID for OPSkins trades on your settings page.
